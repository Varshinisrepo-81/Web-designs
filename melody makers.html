ROCKSTAR ANIRUDH!!

Anirudh Ravichander is known for his keen and dedicated work ethic in the music industry. He pays great attention to detail and experiments with different sounds to suit each film’s mood and story. His passion, consistency, and ability to deliver hit songs in a short time have made him one of the most successful and in-demand music composers in Indian cinema.

ISAIPUYAL A R RAHAMAN!!

A. R. Rahman is known for his keen dedication, discipline, and perfection in music composition. He works with deep focus and constantly experiments with new sounds and technologies to create unique music. His passion, spiritual approach, and commitment to excellence have made him a globally respected music composer.

MELODY KING

G. V. Prakash Kumar is admired for his keen dedication and hard work in the music industry. He is known for his strong understanding of classical and folk music, which he blends beautifully with modern styles. His commitment to creating soulful and meaningful compositions has earned him a special place in the hearts of music lovers.
